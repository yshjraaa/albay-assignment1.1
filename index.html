WELCOME TO ILOCOS!

There is so much to see in the world, come find it with me.

San Agustin Church of Paoay

The Saint Augustine Church, also known as the Paoay Church, is a Roman Catholic church that can be found in the Municipality of Paoay, which is located in the province of Ilocos Norte in the Philippines. The church was finished in 1710 and quickly became well-known for its distinctive architecture, which is best exemplified by the huge buttresses that line the sides and back of the structure.

La Paz Sand Dunes

The La Paz Sand Dunes are a conserved sandy coastal desert and beach that spans 85 square kilometers and can be found in the municipality of Laoag in the province of Ilocos Norte in the Philippines. Recreational pursuits such as sandboarding and driving 4x4 vehicles are extremely well attended to in this region.

Casa Consuelo

People who appreciate the allure of privacy and the privilege of being enveloped in some of nature's most breathtaking settings will find Casa Consuelo to be the ideal tropical retreat for them. The one-of-a-kind huts take their design cues from traditional Ilocano dwellings, but they have all the bells and whistles of a contemporary bathroom. It is located at Pagudpud, Ilocos Norte.

The Cape Bojeador Lighthouse

The Cape Bojeador Lighthouse, often referred to as the Burgos Lighthouse, is a cultural heritage monument that was built in Burgos, Ilocos Norte, during the Spanish Colonial period in the Philippines. It is located in the municipality of Burgos. The lighthouse was turned on for the first time on March 30, 1892. It has been operational for more than a century, and it continues to direct ships that are approaching the Philippine archipelago from the north away from the hazardous rocks that are located along the shore of the town.

The Patapat Bridge

The Patapat Bridge, sometimes referred to as the Patapat Bridge, is a viaduct that can be found in the municipality of Pagudpud, which is located in the province of Ilocos Norte in the Philippines. Pagudpud is a seaside resort town that is located on the most northern tip of the island of Luzon. The height of the bridge, measured in meters above sea level, is 31.

White Rock Formation

White Rock Formation, Kapurpurawan. Ilocos Norte is another Philippine natural masterpiece. The Kapurpurawan Limestone Formation in the province's north is a geological masterpiece. Kapurpurawan Limestone Formation, with its white surface and unique formations, is a must-see in Ilocos Norte.
